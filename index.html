About B&B Architecture

Who We Are

Founded in 2008 by Hanane M.S. Bendraou and Yacine Benouared, B&B Architecture is a leading architectural firm based in Oran. Both founders are graduates of the Institut d’Architecture de l’Université des Sciences et de Technologie d’Oran (USTO).

Our Mission

Our mission is to transform your architectural visions into reality with creativity and precision. We specialize in residential, commercial, and industrial projects, ensuring quality and client satisfaction in every design.

Our Expertise

With over a decade of experience, we offer: Architectural design for various sectors Interior and exterior design Project management and construction supervision 3D visualizations and promotional materials

Our Services

Architecture

At B&B Architecture, we specialize in creating innovative and functional architectural designs. Our services include designing residential homes, commercial spaces, and public facilities. We combine creativity with technical expertise to deliver projects that meet your unique needs and exceed your expectations

Interior Design

B&B Architecture offers bespoke interior design services for both residential and commercial spaces. Our team creates stylish and functional interiors that reflect your personal taste and business identity. From initial concept to final touches, we ensure every detail is perfect to create inviting and inspiring environments.

Exterior Design

B&B Architecture provides expert exterior design services to enhance the aesthetic appeal and functionality of your building's exterior. Our designs integrate seamlessly with the surroundings while showcasing unique architectural elements. We focus on creating durable and visually striking exteriors that make a lasting impression.
